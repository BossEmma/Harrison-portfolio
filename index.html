01 · The Narrative

Forged in Adversity.
Refined by Science.

Born in Warri, Nigeria — the youngest of five to his mother — Harrison's journey was shaped early and profoundly by the loss of his mother at age nine. That crucible forged the independence, endurance, and razor-sharp problem-solving instinct that defines his work today.

Now based in Abuja, FCT, he sits at the intersection of three disciplines: the analytical rigour of Industrial Chemistry, the data precision of a certified analyst, and six years of battle-tested experience navigating global financial markets.

Beyond the markets, Harrison collaborates with technology developers to architect software solutions — viewing artificial intelligence not as a buzzword, but as the most powerful lever available to accelerate Africa's national development, security, and economic transformation.

With a futurist's perspective and a practitioner's discipline, he delivers insight and value to policymakers, business leaders, and institutions seeking clarity in a rapidly shifting world.
